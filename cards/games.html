Games

The games so far are:

  • Nibble
  • Gates

Nibble

Nibble is a black jack style game with differences based on this style deck.

You must stay at 20 or below, and you must have more than the dealer to win.

The risk is much higher the more cards you have since the castles effect the score the more cards you get.

Also, your cost can increase the more cards the dealer gets.

However the reward is greater the more cards you get.

Gates

Uses the logic gate icons to create an interesting twist on Baccarat.

The logic gates are used to calculate score rather than adding cards together.
