play game

Winning

You win by beating the dealer. Highest hand at 20 or below.

Dealer wins all ties.

Calculating you hand score

Use the face value of each card.

For each card you also add the filled in castles. However, castles increase by the power of 2 with each card.

First card: Castles are 1 each Second card: Castles are 2 each Third card: Castles are 4 each Forth card: Castles are 8 each Fifth card: Castles are 16 each sixth card: Castles are 32 each sixth card: Castles are 64 each

It is theoretically you can get more cards, but its highly unlikely.

Natural Wins

If you start with two 0's or two 7's of any castles that is known as a natural win.

(So far) The dealer doesn't win naturally.

Reward

Reward is 'exponential'

You are rewarded by the number of cards in your hand by powers of 2.

Your bet is multiplied by 2 to the (card_count-1)

If you win with 2 cards the payout is 1 times your bet If you win with 3 card the payout is 2 times your bet If you win with 4 card the payout is 4 times your bet If you win with 5 card the payout is 8 times your bet If you win with 6 card the payout is 16 times your bet

Cost

Cost is linear

You are charged by the number of cards in the dealer's hand times 2. If there is no dealer and you're playing the 'dice' dealer. The card count is your hand.

Your bet is multiplied by 2 to the (card_count-1)

If you lose with 2 cards the payout is 1 times your bet If you lose with 3 card the payout is 2 times your bet If you lose with 4 card the payout is 3 times your bet If you lose with 5 card the payout is 4 times your bet
