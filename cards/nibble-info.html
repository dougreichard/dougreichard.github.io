Winning

You win by beating the dealer. Highest hand at 20 or below.

Dealer wins all ties.

Dice vs cards

Two types dealers: Dice vs cards.

With dice 'dealer' is based on rolling dice.

  • Dice 10 rolls 1 D10 and adds 10 to it.
  • Dice 248 rolls a D2 a D4 and a D8

In Dice 10 the dealer cannot bust and will be between 11 and 20 In Dice 248 the dealer can bust and adds the dice to 10. Possible value of 13-24.

With cards, the dealer is dealt a hand an must hit based on the value selected e.g. Hit <14 means the deal must hit on values below 14.

Calculating your hand score

Use the face value of each card.

For each card you also add the filled in castles. However, castles increase by the power of 2 with each card.

  • First card: Castles are 1 each
  • Second card: Castles are 2 each
  • Third card: Castles are 4 each
  • Forth card: Castles are 8 each
  • Fifth card: Castles are 16 each
  • Sixth card: Castles are 32 each
  • Seventh card: Castles are 64 each

It is theoretically possible you can get more cards, but its highly unlikely.

Natural Wins

If you start with two 0's or two 7's of any castles that is known as a natural win.

Natural win pays double.

Reward

Reward is 'exponential'

You are rewarded by the number of cards in your hand by powers of 2.

Your bet is multiplied by 2 to the (card_count-1)

  • If you win with 2 cards the payout is 1 times your bet
  • If you win with 3 card the payout is 2 times your bet
  • If you win with 4 card the payout is 4 times your bet
  • If you win with 5 card the payout is 8 times your bet
  • If you win with 6 card the payout is 16 times your bet

Cost

Cost is linear

You are charged by the number of cards in the dealer's hand times your bet. If there is no dealer and you're playing the 'dice' dealer. The card count is your hand.

Your bet is multiplied by 2 to the (card_count-1)

  • If you lose with 2 cards the payout is 1 times your bet
  • If you lose with 3 card the payout is 2 times your bet
  • If you lose with 4 card the payout is 3 times your bet
  • If you lose with 5 card the payout is 4 times your bet
